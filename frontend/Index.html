What is a dutch auction anyway?

A Dutch auction is a special kind of price-discovery that is frequently employed in financial markets. It begins with a high price and steadily drops until buyer accepts it.

Wikipedia
15 mins read
What is the logic for our dutch auction?

A limited supply of tokens are for sale in each fixed duration auction. The price of tokens begins at an initial price and linearly drops until all tokens are sold or hit minimum price.
